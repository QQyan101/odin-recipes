Ginger Tea

Description

You all know the benefits of ginger tea which I assume is the reason why you are here ...so I won't bother explaining about it. Put all the other benefits aside, the recipe I will be making here is simple and helps with digestion(trust me!).

Ingredients

  • Baby ginger
  • Salt(optional)
  • 2 limes
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Take a small lump of ginger, peel it, and slice it thinly.
  2. Thinly slice your lemons and sundry it.
  3. In a hot, oil-free pan, fry the ginger until it becomes lightly brown.
  4. Next, add a cup of rice and stir to combine.Don't fry it till the rice turns black (we are making ginger tea here, not burned rice tea)

Prepare hot water in your tea pot, add about a handful of your ginger-tea, a pinch of salt and one dried slime and let it steep for about 3 minutes.

And there you have it, a simple ginger tea recipe! You can store it away in a jar and enjoy it whenever you like.
